It's a beautiful thing and a priviledge to be able to bring your creativity to life.

Graphic design was my first dive and passion into tech at the time. I really love the idea of
art and creating imaginative images and objects. It was'nt simple learning/knowing to use most
of the softwares but i always believe if i can accomplish my goals from the most difficult tools

it had to do with alot of thinking and positioning but i practiced more and got better on my skills
and logo designing. As a graphic designer it's required of you to find you're niche in the few aspects
of graphic design and so i found mine in a few including logo designs, branding and print projects.

I love designing, i love being creative and i love being a graphic designer.
