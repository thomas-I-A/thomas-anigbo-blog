MY FIRST YEAR INTO TECH



It all started on a beautiful day. Can't be sure of the specific day or date but it was on a pandemic, the covid-19 pandemic year 2020.

So i recently just got off my job, its been a few months later then the news of the covid-19 virus hit the news. It was on tv and radio stations.
I was so overwhelmed with what was going on, people loosing their loved ones to the virus, restriction of movement, curphew and also that i'll
have to wait till the virus has been wiped off and a declaration of free movement before i can get a job or atleast seek for one.

I really really got bored staying home and indoors and this is for a person that barely keeps alot of friends but guess what, it wasn't just me,
it was a whole world problem. Alot of people were also bored so social media got more exciting, alot of friends reached out on video calls, chats and
group calls. Socialmedia kinda made staying home a little fun and those who had neighbours they liked or as friends really got the opportunity to
get along. Somehow someday i was on socialmedia and i came across a design i liked and i've always been looking for what to do to get creative and
seize the opportunity of staying at home, then i started diving deep, lurking pages with designs that i admired and there and then i started getting
creative ,taking classes and little by little i went from being just a graphic designer to a programmer. I found my niche and ever since i have loved
my progress as a graphics designer and a web developer open to colaborate, learn more and build more.
